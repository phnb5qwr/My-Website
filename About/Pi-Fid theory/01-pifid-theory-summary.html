📘 PiFid Theory — 1‑Page Summary What is the PiFid Theory? A three‑layer collision model of Cultural Zones × Algorithm × Platform Structure on social media. Conflicts on SNS are not caused by “personality,” but by the collision of different cultural zones, algorithmic misplacement, and platform‑level structures. 1. SNS Has Distinct “Cultural Zones” (Pi’s Insight) SNS is not “a group of people who speak the same language.” It is a world where multiple cultural zones with different values coexist. Main cultural zones: Quiet Zone — calm, polite, peaceful Flame Zone — reaction, speed, confrontation Debate Zone — logic, counterarguments, discussion Tech Zone — systems, analysis, structure Life Zone — daily life, empathy Creative Zone — expression, aesthetics → Conflicts arise when cultural zones collide. 2. Algorithms Do Not Understand Cultural Zones (Fid’s Analysis) SNS algorithms look only at numbers, not culture. Fast initial engagement High reactions “Interesting” signals These can cause a post to be thrown into the Flame Zone. → A Quiet‑Zone user may suddenly be dropped into the Flame Zone. → What is “normal greeting” in the Flame Zone feels like a “disaster” to the Quiet Zone. 3. Platform Operators Stand Outside All Cultural Zones The platform is neither Quiet, nor Flame, nor Debate. Operators are the ones who build the stage itself. And because: Flames → more reactions Reactions → longer watch time Watch time → more ad revenue → The Flame Zone and platform interests can align. → But this does not mean the platform “supports” flaming. 4. The Flame Zone’s “Peace” Is the Quiet Zone’s “Disaster” Flame Zone norms: Quick reactions (courtesy) Teasing / playful jabs (communication) Storm‑like threads (group energy) Thorough counterarguments (culture) No weakness allowed (aesthetic) Quiet Zone norms: Politeness Calm tone Peace Non‑interference → Their values are opposite. 5. Correct Actions During Trouble Do not force yourself to fight Leave when you sense a cultural mismatch Block / mute is not “running away” but self‑protection Return to your own cultural zone → SNS outcomes are determined by cultural compatibility, not winning or losing. 6. The Core of PiFid Theory SNS conflicts are not about personality. They arise from the three‑layer collision of: Cultural Zones Algorithmic Misplacement Platform Structure Understanding this explains 90% of SNS trouble. 7. Why This Theory Is Necessary Because misunderstandings, cultural clashes, algorithmic misplacement, and misconceptions about platform operators cause conflicts where no one is actually at fault. This theory is needed for both teenagers and adults.

SNSには“文化圏”がある（Piの洞察） SNSは「同じ日本語を話す人の集まり」ではなく、 価値観の違う文化圏が混ざった世界。 主な文化圏： 静か圏：平和・丁寧・穏やか 炎上圏：刺激・反応・対立・スピード 議論圏：論理・反論・討論 技術圏：仕組み・分析・構築 生活圏：日常・共感 創作圏：表現・感性 → 文化圏の違いが衝突を生む。 2. アルゴリズムは文化圏を理解しない（Fidの分析） SNSのアルゴリズムは 「数字」だけを見て投稿を動かす。 初速が伸びた 面白い 反応が多い こういう投稿を 炎上圏に投げ込むことがある。 → 静か圏の人が突然炎上圏に放り込まれる（誤配属）。 → 炎上圏の“挨拶”が静か圏には“災害”に見える。 3. プラットフォーム運営側は文化圏の“外側” 運営側は 静か圏でも 炎上圏でも 議論圏でもない。 舞台そのものを作る存在。 さらに、 炎上＝反応が増える 反応＝滞在時間が伸びる 滞在時間＝広告収益が増える → 炎上圏と運営側は利害関係でつながる。 → しかし「炎上を応援している」わけではない。 4. 炎上圏の“平和”は静か圏の“災害” 炎上圏の通常運転： 反応を返す（礼儀） いじり・からかい（コミュニケーション） 嵐（ノリ） 反論は徹底的に（文化） 弱音NG（美学） 静か圏の通常運転： 丁寧 落ち着き 平和 干渉しない → 価値観が真逆。 5. トラブル時の正しい行動 無理に戦わない 文化圏が違うと感じたら離れる ブロック・ミュートは“逃げ”ではなく“安全行動” 自分の文化圏に合う場所に戻る → SNSは勝ち負けではなく、文化圏の相性で決まる。 6.
